About Connor ESL

Hi! My name is Connor and I am originally from California in the United States. I am in my second year of teaching and I have been very excited on this journey. It gives me great pleasure to know that I am sharing my knowledge from my native language to connect people from around the world.

Before I traveled here, I received a level-5 TEFL certification. I have been teaching students of all ages from kindergarten to sixth grade and Cambridge exam preparation classes from levels A2 to C1. I specialize in speaking and conversational practice as well as grammar and pronunciation refinement.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, traveling, skiing, and going to music festivals. As far as traveling, I deeply enjoy going to new places and learning about many different cultures.

Why Choose Me?

  • Native English speaker from California
  • TEFL Level-5 certified
  • Over 2 years teaching experience online
  • Personalized lessons focused on speaking and grammar
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your time zone

I believe learning English should be engaging and fun, while improving fluency and confidence. Whether you are a beginner or advanced learner, my lessons are designed to meet your needs.
